Travel Risks

Travelers who choose to visit these planets through space transportataion are at risk of the following:

  • muscle atrophy
  • radiation
  • illness susceptibility
  • exhaustion

What to Pack

In order to mitigate these risks, it's best to pack:

  • first aid kit
  • space suit
  • air-sealed suitcase
  • disposable snacks
  • bottled water

Recommended Space Lines
